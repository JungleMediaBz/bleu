OUR STORY

Bleu Bakery is a boutique café & bakery located in Belize City, offering freshly-baked artisan breads, pastries, coffee, breakfast items, and specialty drinks. We aim to deliver high-quality, thoughtfully-crafted baked goods in a welcoming café atmosphere that blends modern café culture with local Belizean charm.

Einar Marin, Founder

Founded and led by Einar Marin, the bakery was born from a vision to bring artisan bakery and café vibes to Belize City. Einar's passion for quality ingredients and the craft of baking is at the heart of everything we do, from our daily-fresh breads to our creative drink lineup.
